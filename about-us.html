About us

History

We love ducks. We also love fika. That's why we created Ankademin Cafe in 2020, a place where we can combine the things we love.

Inspired by the cat cafees in Japan we thought we could do a similar thing with ducks and that's how we got started.

By now we are very successful thanks to our loyal customers. Thank you and we hope to see you in Ankademin Cafe soon!

Motto

Out of luck? Pet a duck.

The above line is our company motto. If you're feeling down or just out of luck, one thing that is sure to improve your mood is petting an animal. In our case, a duck.

Fill your stomach by enjoying our fika and fill your soul by petting our ducks. That's all there is to it!
